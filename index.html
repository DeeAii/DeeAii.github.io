Frequently
asked question

How much is the application fee?

LTW services are free with no claims on intellectual property or equity position.

Are new companies accepted?

LTW accepts companies with a good opportunity and enhances their capacity to sell to first customers, raise funds, attract and retain talent. We ensure our portfolio companies get the support for rapid and early access to a global market.

What is unique about the learning resources?

LTW offers practical experience learning from entrepreneurship research by academic and industry experts with high-impact coaching from successful entrepreneurs.
